Delmont, Matthew. “African-Americans Fighting Fascism and Racism, from WWII to Charlottesville.” The Conversation, Arizona State University, 18 Apr. 2018, theconversation.com/african-americans-fighting-fascism-and-racism-from-wwii-to-charlottesville-82551. included great notes for my background notes
History.com Staff. “The Tuskegee Airmen: 5 Fascinating Facts.” History.com, A+E Television Networks, 20 Jan. 2012, www.history.com/news/the-tuskegee-airmen-5-fascinating-facts. Interesting fun facts on the Tuskegee Airmen and how the 1st Lady helped with allowing them to fly.
History.com Staff. “Tuskegee Airmen.” History.com, A+E Television Networks, 2009, www.history.com/topics/world-war-ii/tuskegee-airmen. Broader facts on the Tuskegee airmen and facts about them and their history
Little, Becky. “Lost Tuskegee Airman's Body May Have Been Found.” History.com, A&E Television Networks, 10 Apr. 2018, www.history.com/news/tuskegee-airman-discovery-73-years-after-wwii. Interesting facts on the discovery of a Tuskegee airmen being found in Australia
Parscale. “African Americans in WWII.” African Americans in WWII | Fredericksburg War Museum, 2018, www.pacificwarmuseum.org/your-visit/african-americans-in-wwii/. Facts on the African American infantry in WWII and not just on the Tuskegee Airmen.
